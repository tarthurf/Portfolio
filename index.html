About Me

Hello, my name is Tyler Arthur and I am a web developer. This is my portfolio, a place where I keep my projects for anyone who is interested to look at. This website started out without much of anything in it, but as I learn and grow so will my projects as and their complexity. I don’t have a set idea of what I would like to do as a web developer but I feel like that gives me an advantage. I don’t have a preordained path that I want to follow and often times wandering can lead you to wondrous places you never thought you would end up.

I have worked in many fields up until now including clothing retail, HVAC sales, delivery driver, beer distribution, computer manufacturing, and I even had a short stint of employment in an adult entertainment store (head over to the contact page if you want to hear some stories). On top of those, I served in the Army National Guard for 7 years and also run a small dog sitting business.

Many avenues of my life have led me to web development, mainly my love of problem solving, tinkering with software, messing around with different Linux distros, and jail-breaking phones and tablets. I also am an avid player of tabletop role-playing games like Dungeons and Dragons and often run games and build the worlds my players reside in from scratch. I am also an active person but above all enjoy rock climbing indoors and out, whenever I get a chance.
